TAKEAWAY & RESTAURANT + 2 FLATS (2-BED FLAT & 1-BED STUDIO FLAT)

A spacious premises measuring approximately 1,656 sq. ft. (154 sq. m.), currently trading as a Peri Peri chicken shop, but would suit other various cuisine types having previously traded as a fish & chip shop. Takings average £6,000 per week and that is still growing over our client's period of ownership, with projections that this will continue to grow as the business becomes more established.

Rent £22,800 per annum which includes the shop, 2-bed flat above and 1-bed studio flat above. The 2-bed flat is currently sub-let at £1,000 pcm (£12,000 pa) and the 1-bed studio flat is currently sub-let at £600 pcm (£7,200 pa) leaving just £3,600 per annum payable for the shop.

Viewing recommended.
