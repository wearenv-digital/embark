Why invest in Greenfield or Brownfield land in the UK with Embark?

The UK is one of the most sought-after places in the world to invest in land, with growth rates that are significantly higher than other property markets. Land for property development is an effective investment vehicle for both individuals and businesses as it offers excellent long-term returns on investment with high yields.

Our land development specialists are privileged to offer a wide range of land development services, for both seasoned and new property investors. Our experience allows us to identify areas that are expected to increase in value rapidly, and we can guide you through the entire process from cradle (the initial purchase of land) to grave (successfully selling on your investment).

With over a decade of experience in the development sector, our property developers are determined to ensure our clients are able make informed decisions at each stage of the investment journey.

Why invest in land development now?

The UK's current economic climate is ideal for investing in land and property. A combination of high-yield rental yields, robust capital growth and some of the highest rates of tenant demand in the country make it one of the most attractive markets in Europe.

Whether you are an overseas investor looking to add to your portfolio, or a UK-based beginner investor interested in making your first property investment purchase, our property developers can help you.

At Embark property development group, we appreciate that buying or selling a plot of land can be daunting, especially if you are new to this side of the property market. That's why our dedicated and passionate team are at hand 7 days a week to help you find the ideal land for property development, forecast the next stages in your land planning journey and source ideal residential development land, with your return on investment firmly in mind.
